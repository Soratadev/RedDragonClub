Welcome to the Red Dragon Club


Here you can find information about the board games you like the most and book one of our tables to have a good time with friends or other club members.


Remember that to enjoy our games and booking services you must register and log in.


Our catalog

In our catalog you can find a wide variety of board games, from the most classic to the most modern and complex. You can search for games by name, designer, number of players, playing time, category, complexity, age, and cover image. If you don't find the game you are looking for, don't hesitate to let a member of staff know.
